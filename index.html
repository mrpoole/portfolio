MICHELLE POOLE

WEB DEVELOPER

Just a human writing code in Orange County.

I got my start in web development as a kid frankencoding Myspace and Geocities layouts together. I never lost the love of hunting for lost semicolons, but I spent several years honing my personal skills in the service industry before I made the difficult decision to leave the world of coffee and craft cocktails to focus on studying programming full-time.

It's hands down the best decision I've ever made.

I love being part of an industry that has creation and problem-solving at its core. Being able to work in a team to build something that wouldn't be possible on your own never gets old, and I look forward to honing my skills at building clean, efficient, and reusable code.
